future space definer 未来空间产品定义者

朴隅建筑PURE Achitects是总部成立于深圳，在上海、成都及重庆设有全资子公司的建筑设计事务所。公司合伙人具备丰富的建筑设计及项目管理经验。业务范围覆盖城市规划、TOD、商业综合体、超高层、酒店、养老、办公、居住、室内、景观等领域。朴隅以“设计+管理”为核心服务模式，为业主提供包含前期策划、全程设计、设计管理、建设管理等多维度、一站式服务。朴隅用专业提供创造性的解决方案，用管理实现设计的整体交付。朴隅的专业价值和管理经验成功地整合了多家海内外知名设计咨询资源，为业主需求提供定制化的服务。

Headquartered in Shenzhen, Pure Architects has subsidiary design offices in Shanghai, Chengdu and Chongqing. Established by the partners with extensive experience in architectural design and project management, the company provides design services in urban planning, TOD (Transit-Oriented-Development), business complex, super high-rise building, hospitality, elderly care, office, residential building, interior design, landscape architecture, etc.

Based on the service mode ‘Design&Management’ , our company provides clients with one-stop and multidimensional services, including early planning, architectural design in the whole process, design management and construction management. Creative design solutions are given by our absolute profession; the deliveries of the whole architectural design are realized by our quality management. Combined with many renowned design consulting resources, Pure Architects provide our clients with customized services by professional value and management experience.

    innovation
    职能创新

    朴隅建筑希望用自身的实践去探索建筑师的工作边界。我们成立了自己的项目管理公司，介入建筑全过程的设计管理，让设计理念从建筑酝酿的初期就得以切入，从只知伏案绘图的“一支笔”转变为实现设计最终价值的“产品经理”。
    In Pure Architects, the work limit boundary is explored with practices by all our architects. Our own project management subsidiary company is established to participate in the whole process of design management so that the design concept can be maintained throughout the project. Meanwhile, the design role transition from the individual architectural designer to the whole design manager achieving the final project value is realized.

  • business
    innovation
    业务创新

    我们主动研究社群的需求，以需求为导向去延伸我们的设计和服务。我们不仅为设计师创造开放、协作、激发创造力的创作空间，同时，我们也主动根据个性化的市场需要，主动联合有共同价值观的室内、景观、工程、灯光、视觉等设计资源去定制个性化的产品，创造更加贴近终端客户细微需求的项目。
    We actively research different demands of social groups, and extend our design and service with the demands as the guidance. Not only do we provide our architects with open work space where they can work together and be inspired, but also offer the personalized services to meet the market needs in cooperation with other design resources, such as interior design, landscape design, engineers, lighting design and visual design. All these efforts are in order to create the project which meets the demands of terminal consumers more suitable.
